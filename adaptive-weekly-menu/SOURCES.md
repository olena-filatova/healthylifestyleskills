# Sources

The dietary guidance in this skill is grounded in evidence-based recommendations from the following organisations and publications.

## Diabetes

- **Diabetes UK** — dietary guidance for Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes, carbohydrate management, low-GI eating
  https://www.diabetes.org.uk/guide-to-diabetes/enjoy-food/eating-with-diabetes

- **NHS — Type 1 diabetes and food** — carb counting and meal timing guidance
  https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/type-1-diabetes/living-with-type-1-diabetes/diet-and-exercise/

## Cholesterol

- **British Heart Foundation** — practical dietary advice on reducing LDL cholesterol
  https://www.bhf.org.uk/informationsupport/heart-matters-magazine/nutrition/cholesterol/10-ways-to-reduce-your-cholesterol

- **NHS — High cholesterol** — saturated fat, soluble fibre, and omega-3 guidance
  https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/high-cholesterol/how-to-lower-your-cholesterol/

## Perimenopause

- **British Menopause Society** — nutrition guidance for perimenopause and menopause
  https://thebms.org.uk/publications/tools-for-clinicians/diet-and-lifestyle-in-the-menopause/

- **NHS — Menopause** — general lifestyle and dietary recommendations
  https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/menopause/

## General Nutrition

- **British Dietetic Association (BDA)** — evidence-based healthy eating principles
  https://www.bda.uk.com/resource/healthy-eating.html

- **NHS Eatwell Guide** — baseline dietary framework used across UK nutrition guidance
  https://www.nhs.uk/live-well/eat-well/food-guidelines-and-food-labels/the-eatwell-guide/
